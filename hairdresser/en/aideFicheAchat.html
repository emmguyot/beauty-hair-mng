Help for Purchase items

This page lets you quickly input your order of items you make to your suppliers. Possible actions are :

  • Input reception of an order :
    • Select the supplier from which the order come from,
    • Input the order number or the invoice number,
    • Input the kind of buy move you want to use,
    • Select the category of the received item on the green working line,
    • Select the received item on the green workinf line,
    • Input the quantity and the price net of tax that are shown on your invoice,
    • Click on to create the buy move for the data you've entered and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
    • Start over as long as necessary.
  • Correct an error in your input :
    • Click on to remove the line with the error
    • Input once again the ligne as described above
  • Display or modify the information for an item (Supplier part) :
    • Click on the name of the item to display the Item form with its suppliers.
  • Display or modify the information for an item (Move part) :
    • Click on the date of the last move for the item you're interested in to display the Item form with its last moves

You can check the hints and tips too.
