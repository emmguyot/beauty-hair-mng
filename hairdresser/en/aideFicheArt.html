Help for the Item form

This page presents information for an item that you have in your stock. This page is articulated around two shutters: Suppliers of the article or Inventory turnovers of the article.

  • To create an item :
    • Choose the kind of item (You will be able to invoice only on the articles declared on "Detail sale")
    • Choose the category of the article
    • Input the wording of the article
    • Input the usual reference from the suppliers
    • Input the stock below which an order is necessary
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To quickly create several articles :
    • Create the first article as usual
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
    • Modify the data for the next article
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
    • Start over as long as necessary starting from duplication
  • To modify main information of the article, the already declared suppliers :
    • Input or modify the information
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To post information of the supplier of the article in order to contact it :
    • Click on the name of the supplier in blue, the supplier form then appears
  • To add a supplier for this article :
    • In the green working line, choose the corresponding supplier
    • Input the wording if it is different from yours
    • Input the reference of the article in the supplier
    • Input the minimal quantity to order
    • Input the unit price net of tax corresponding to this minimal quantity
    • Choose if this supplier is the supplier privileged for this article
    • Validate the line with and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To remove a supplier for this item :
    • Click on in the line you want to remove and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To add a stock move for your item :
    • In the green working line, choose a kind of move (The orientation of the move Input, Output, Inventory is displayed in the brackets)
    • Input the quantity of the move
    • For input moves (e.g.: purchase) ou for the inventory moves, input the unit value net of tax of the move.
    • Validate the line with and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To delete the last move (Typo error) :
    • Click on of the line to remove and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To remove an item :
    • The item must not have been used in an invoice, a service, or a stock move
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,

You can check the hints and tips too.
