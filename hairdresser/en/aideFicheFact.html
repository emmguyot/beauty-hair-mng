Help for the invoice form

This form displays information about an invoice : The lines of service or product sell, the heading of the invoice and the foot which includes payment information.

  • To modify the heading, the foot or a line of service or a product sell :
    • Enter data
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To modify or enter technical information for a service :
    • Click on the icon or in the line
    • Enter the comment in the field that opens next to your click
    • Confirm the comment
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To add a line of service or a product sell :
    • In the green working line, choose the kind of service
    • Choose the catalog category or the brand of the product
    • Choose the catalog item
    • Choose the collaborator that has done the service or the sell
    • You may modify the quantity
    • Click in the icon
    • Enter the technical information for the service
    • Confirm the line with and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To remove a line of service or a product sell :
    • Click on of the line to remove and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To print a bill :
    • Click on when you've entered all the necessary information and when the payment is selected. It also saves the modifications you've made.
  • To print the check for your customer :
    • Click on "Print the check" near the payment. This link is only available if the invoice has been paid by check
  • If your customer pays several invoices in one shot, he's doing a grouped payments :
    • Click on "Grouped payments" near the payment to display the payment form. This in only available if the invoice hasn't been paid yet, or if the grouped payment already group invoices.
  • To cancel a payment :
    • If the invoice has been paid alone, enter a blank payment
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title, The asset and the stocks are updated to cancel the previous moves made.
  • To delete an invoice (normal case) :
    • If necessary, cancel the payment of the invoice
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To delete a paid invoice (special operation) :
    • Click on
    • Read the warning message and enter the password for special operations
    • Click on to make the deletion effective and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To display the customer form for the current customer :
    • Click on the name of the customer that is shown in blue, the customer form displays.
  • To display the service form from on line of the invoice (eg: To change a price) :
    • Click on the blue text of the service or the product sell, The catalog entry form is displayed.

You can check the hints and tips too.
