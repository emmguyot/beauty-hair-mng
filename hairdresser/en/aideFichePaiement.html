Help for the payment form

This form displays information about a payment that has been done for one or more invoices : The upper part displays the payment information and the main part displays the invoices (those that has to be paid and those that has been paid with this payment).

  • To pay several invoices at once :
    • Enter or change the payment
    • Check the invoices
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To print a bill :
    • Click on when every information is correct. It will save you modification at the same time.
  • To display the customer form of one of those displayed in the list :
    • Click on the name displayed in blue
  • To display an invoice that is in the list :
    • Click on the date of the invoice
  • To print the check for your customer :
    • Click on "Check print" near the total. This is only available when the payment has been saved
  • To change the payment after you've saved it :
    • Modify the payment
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title, The cash assets and the stocks has been modified in order to cancel the previous payment and take into account the new one.
  • To remove a payment :
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title, The cash assets and the stocks has been modified in order to cancel the previous payment The invoices are now back in the "current" ones.

You can check the hints and tips too.
