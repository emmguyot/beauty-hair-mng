Help for the list

The list displays data in a table. Above the table, you may find criterea (Letters, dates, ...) to filter the table. As soon as a field is modified, the table is updated.

Each line contains at least one blue link. It lets you display the form in order to check or modify data.

At the bottom of the screen, in the available actions, you may create a new form with the button New.

Reminder :

  • On every screen, you may print it with a click with of the right button of the mouse and select Print
  • You can get displayed information in another software through the Copy / Paste mechanism. First select the part you're interested in (Left click and drag), the copy (Right click and select Copy). Now, you may paste in another software such as a text writer or a spreadsheet. This is useful to create your paper catalog ou print your stocks for inventory

 

You can check the hints and tips too.
