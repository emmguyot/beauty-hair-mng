Help for the item inventory form

This form displays the list of items according to your criterea. It lets you :

  • Enter the inventory of your items :
    • Select the kind of inventory moves to use
    • Enter for each item you've counted the quantity and the value associated if it is different from the previous one
    • Click on to create the inventory moves and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • Read or modify the suppliers information for one item :
    • Click on the name of the item to display the item form with its suppliers
  • Read or modify the move information for one item :
    • Click on the date of the last move for the item you're interested in to display the item form with the latest moves
  • Filter the items list :
    • Choose a kind of item to display only this kind of items
    • Choose an item category to display only items belonging to that category
    • Both filters may be used.

To enter the full inventory, It is a good idea to save frequently to prevent data loss if you make mistakes in your moves.

You can check the hints and tips too.
