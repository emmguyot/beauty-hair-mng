Help for the customer form

This page shows customer's information in order to consult or modify them. Only the 10 first services are shown when the page is displayed.

The mandatory data are shown with stand up letters and optional data are shown in italic. So, to create a customer, you only have to enter his name. Anyway we recommend you to enter as much information as possible in order to do mailings, statistics... Moreover, the comment field lets you keep track of some key informations about your customer (general technical information, general information, ...)

  • To change the customer's information :
    • Enter or modify the information
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To display the whole invoice history for the customer :
    • Click on "Every services"
  • To welcome a customer in the application for a specific service :
    • Click on the icon in front of the service you have to do. This action duplicate the invoice and place the new one in the Current list.
  • To filter the customer history according to the kind of service :
    • Choose the kind of service you're interested in in the combo box near "Service"
  • To create customer history before you've started to use the software :
    • Click on "New history"
    • The invoice form displays a blank invoice for history
  • To modify technical data or to add how the customer evaluate your service :
    • Click on the comment to modify or on the icon in the history part
    • Enter the comment in the field that pops up
    • Validate the comment
  • To print the technical data :
    • Click on "Print the technical data"
    • The technical data are technical information you need for future services. It allows you to print them.
  • To read back the details of one customer's invoice :
    • Click on the date of the invoice you want to display among those in the history.
  • To send an email to the customer :
    • Click on the icon
    • Fill in your message
    • Don't forget to connect to Internet
  • To remove a customer :
    • Check that the customer has no invoice
    • Click on and verify that all is correct by reading the message below the title,
  • To get back to the customer list and display another customer :
    • Click on to display the customer list

You can check the hints and tips too.
