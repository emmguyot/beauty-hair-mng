Hints and tips

  • To go from one field to another : The Tab key is fast and efficient. In the other way, use the Shift key with the Tab : You'll go to the previous field.
  • In a combo box, press the first letter to select the first word that starts with this letter. A new key press select the 2nd one and so on... So combined with the use of the mouse, you earn precious time in huge lists.
  • In a field, to change the whole value, use the keys Control (Ctrl) with the A key, then enter the new value : You'll earn time again.
  • On every screen, you may print it with a click with of the right button of the mouse and select Print
  • You can get displayed information in another software through the Copy / Paste mechanism. First select the part you're interested in (Left click and drag), the copy (Right click and select Copy). Now, you may paste in another software such as a text writer or a spreadsheet. This is useful to create your paper catalog ou print your stocks for inventory
  • If when you open LibertyLook, the first page doesn't display in full screen, you may swith to full screen with the F11 key.
